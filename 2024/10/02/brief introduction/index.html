Welcome to Urban Air Laboratory (UAL)!

Our group primarily focuses on urban meteorology and air pollution, specifically examining multi-scale urban atmospheric flows, turbulence transport, pollutant dispersion, and atmospheric organic pollutants. Our research aims to provide scientific support for sustainable urban development, climate change mitigation, air quality improvement, and low-altitude flight safety. We hope to advance the optimization of urban environments and contribute to the formulation of effective policies to address the growing challenges of urban meteorology and pollution.

我们组的主要研究方向是城市气象和空气污染，具体聚焦于多尺度城市大气流动、湍流传输、88污染物扩散以及大气有机污染物等关键问题。我们的研究旨在为可持续城市建设、气候变化应对、空气质量提升及低空飞行安全保障等领域提供科学依据和支持。我们期望能够推动城市环境的优化和相关政策的制定，以应对日益严峻的城市气象和污染挑战。
